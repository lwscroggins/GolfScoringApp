A few notes before you go on

  • This is the most basic working version I could make
  • Do NOT look at this on a desktop. It's made for your phone; please use it as intended
  • From right here, click on the menu and select "Select Course"
  • Do what seems natural on that page (that one is kind of intuitive)
  • Pebble Beach is the shortest example (only 3 holes). The others both have 18
  • Press 'Fore!' to start a game
  • For each hole, enter a score (like you would if you were actually playing) and swipe the box from left to right
  • Bad things may happen if you swipe right to left. Honestly, I don't know. I didn't tell it to do anything in that case
  • When you're done scoring, hit the 'End Game' button
